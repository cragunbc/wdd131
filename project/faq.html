View our page about local clubs!

How often to people play?

Most people play multiple times a week but based on availability some people can only play once or twice a week. Really just depends on people schedules!

Where are the local courts?

  • Syracuse: Founders Park
  • Layton: Ellison Park
  • Clinton: Steed Park
  • Farmington: Farmington Park
  • Kaysville: Barnes Park

Can you get hurt playing pickleball?

Like any sport you can get hurt. Pickleball is no different; just make sure the stretch and not to play harder then you are physically able to and you should be fine. Make sure to have fun!
